Everything you need to know about joining the team, sponsoring us, or following our season.

How do I join the team?
+
Recruitment opens each fall semester. We welcome students from all disciplines — engineering, business, and design. No prior motorsport experience is needed, just a passion for building things and working in a team environment.
What competitions do you attend?
+
We primarily compete at Formula SAE Lincoln, the largest FSAE event in the US. We also pursue international competitions including Formula Student events in England, Australia, and Japan — where we've won championships in all three.
How can a company sponsor us?
+
We offer tiered sponsorship packages with branding on the car and team kit, event presence, recruiting access to 80+ engineering students, and featured placement on this site. Visit the Sponsors page or reach out directly for our prospectus.
Combustion or electric?
+
Both. We run the F26 combustion car and are actively developing the E25 and E27 electric platforms for competition in the FSAE Electric class. 2026 is our first season fielding both programs simultaneously.
What subteams can I join?
+
Aerodynamics, chassis, suspension, drivetrain, engine, battery pack, electronics, data acquisition, composites, ergonomics, cost, and business — each with dedicated leads and real hands-on engineering ownership from day one.
